Essential Safety Tips

Public Transport

Key Precautions

  • Always share ride details with trusted contacts
  • Pre-book verified cabs at night
  • Avoid empty compartments in trains/buses
  • Use women-only coaches when available

Night Safety

Important Measures

  • Carry pepper spray/personal alarm
  • Walk in well-lit areas facing traffic
  • Avoid phone distraction while walking
  • Trust your instincts - if unsafe, enter nearest public place

Digital Safety

Cyber Security

  • Never share personal details online
  • Use strong unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Report cyber crimes immediately

Home Safety

Domestic Precautions

  • Install peepholes in doors
  • Keep emergency numbers on speed dial
  • Use door chains for unknown visitors
  • Maintain digital records of domestic helps

Workplace Safety

Office Guidelines

  • Report harassment immediately to ICC
  • Avoid isolated meetings after hours
  • Use company transport for late shifts
  • Know your POSH Committee members

General Precautions

Always Remember

  • Trust your instincts
  • Learn basic self-defense
  • Share live location with trusted contacts
  • Memorize emergency numbers
